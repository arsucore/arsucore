### 🕹️ About Me

I'm a high school student who's into **web development** and **cybersecurity** at the same time. I like the whole process — writing something that works, then figuring out how it could be broken, then figuring out how to stop that. Still early in the journey, still learning fast, still building whatever comes to mind.

I'm not chasing "expert" status yet. I'm chasing reps — the more I build and break stuff, the faster I actually get better.

### 🎈 What Drives Me

I like making things and then poking holes in them to see how they hold up. Writing code that works feels good, but understanding *why* something broke feels even better. I'm not trying to know everything right now — I'm trying to build the habit of learning something new every week, not just when there's homework due.

Building and securing feel like two ends of the same skill to me — if you can build it, you should understand how someone could mess with it too.
