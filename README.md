### 🚀 About Me

I'm a high school student who fell in love with tech early — currently exploring **web development** and **cybersecurity** side by side. I like understanding how things are built, and just as much, how they can be broken and defended. Every project I start teaches me something new about writing code, and every bug I chase teaches me something new about thinking like an attacker.

I'm not "finished learning" anything yet — and that's the fun part. I'm early in the journey, moving fast, and building a foundation that covers both **creating** software and **securing** it.

### 💡 What Drives Me

I think the best way to learn tech is to actually build with it — so I try to turn what I learn in class and online into real, working projects. I'm especially drawn to cybersecurity because it makes me think differently: not just "does this work," but "how could this be broken, and how do I stop that."

I'm not chasing perfection right now — I'm chasing reps. The more I build, break, and fix, the faster I get better.
